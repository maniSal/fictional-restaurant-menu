Let's tast algerian food !

Appetizers

  • Bourak

    Thin pastry sheets filled with meat ,cheese and potatoes .

  • hmiss

    Algerian salad made primarily from roasted vegetables .

  • Lham Lahlou

    A sweet and savory dish of dried fuits cooked in a syrupy sauce with cinnamon sugar and orange blossom with meat.

Main Courses

  • Rechta

    Small flat noodles served with sauce and chickpeas.

  • Metaouem

    Meatballs cooked in a garlicky,spiced sauce.

  • Chorba Frik

    A light soup made with crushed green wheat , vetebables and meat.

Desserts

  • Baghrir

    Spongy semolina pancakes with multiple holes on the surface , served with honey and butter.

  • Kalb El Louz

    A most semolina cake layered with almond paste,flavored with orange blossom water.

  • Mkhabaz

    An almond-based pastry flavored with lemon or orange blossom water,covered with powdered sugar.
